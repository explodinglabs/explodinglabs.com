Natural scrolling in Xorg

Beau Barker, Jul 20, 2016. Updated Jul 30, 2025. ✎

Edit /etc/X11/xorg.conf.d/50-synaptics.conf:

Section "InputClass"
    ...
    Option "HorizScrollDelta" "-111"
    Option "VertScrollDelta" "-111"
EndSection
