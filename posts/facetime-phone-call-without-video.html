How to make a FaceTime phone call without video on a Mac?

Beau Barker, Feb 28, 2024. Updated Jul 16, 2025. ✎

To make a FacteTime phone call without video on a Mac:

  1. Start FaceTime
  2. Click “New FaceTime”
  3. Enter the phone number and press Enter.
  4. Click the arrow that appears next to the phone number, and under Call using iPhone, click the phone number.

The audio settings can be set under System Settings > Sound.
