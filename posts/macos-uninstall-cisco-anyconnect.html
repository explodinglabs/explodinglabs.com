How to uninstall Cisco Anyconnect on MacOS?

Beau Barker, Dec 18, 2020. Updated Jul 15, 2025. ✎

As root, execute

/opt/cisco/vpn/bin/vpn_uninstall.sh

or

/System/Volumes/Data/opt/cisco/anyconnect/bin/vpn_uninstall.sh
