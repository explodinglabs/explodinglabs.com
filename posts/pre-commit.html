How to upgrade versions in Pre-commit?

Beau Barker, Feb 2, 2021. Updated Jul 15, 2025. ✎
  1. Change the version in .pre-commit-config.yaml.
  2. Run pre-commit autoupdate.

Next time you commit, the package will upgrade.
